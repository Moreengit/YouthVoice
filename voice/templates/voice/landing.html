YouthVoice

Connecting Residents to their Leaders

SDG 16: Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions

YouthVoice is a web platform that connects Kenyan youth directly with their local leaders at the ward level. Users register as Resident or Leader, select their exact location, post community ideas with photos, vote in real-time, and engage in threaded discussions. Leaders see only ideas from their ward, can mark them as Open/In-Progress/Done, and post official responses.

About

YouthVoice is built to empower Kenyan youth by bridging the gap between residents and their local leaders. It provides a transparent, structured, and interactive way for communities to voice concerns, propose solutions, and collaborate with ward representatives. The platform promotes accountability, civic education, and participatory governance—ensuring every voice contributes to shaping a better future.

Our Vision

To become Kenya’s leading digital civic engagement platform where youth actively participate in shaping community development and public service delivery.

Our Mission

To empower young people with digital tools that enhance accountability, civic participation, and collaboration between citizens and leaders.

Our Values

Transparency, inclusion, innovation, and community-centered leadership drive every feature and project within YouthVoice.
